Keto Guidelines

  • Net Carbs < 20g/day — Total carbs minus fiber. This is the key number for ketosis.
  • Fat = 70–80% of calories — Fat is your primary fuel source on keto.
  • Protein = 15–25% — ~0.7–1g per lb of lean body mass. Too much can raise blood sugar.
  • Stay hydrated — Drink 8+ cups of water. Electrolytes (sodium, potassium, magnesium) are essential.
  • Your goal: 185 lbs — A typical deficit of 500 kcal/day leads to ~1 lb/week loss.
  • Keto flu — First 1–2 weeks may feel rough. Push through — it's temporary electrolyte depletion.
  • Best keto foods — Meat, fish, eggs, cheese, avocado, low-carb vegetables, nuts, olive oil.
